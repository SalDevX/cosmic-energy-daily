Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026. This policy covers what information we collect when you sign up for the Cosmic Energy Daily newsletter and how we use it.

What we collect

We collect your email address and your zodiac sign. That's it. No name, no location, no device fingerprint, no tracking pixels.

How we use it

Your email is used solely to send you the Cosmic Energy Daily newsletter — weekly horoscope readings, lunar phase alerts, and cosmic event notes. We will never use it to send ads, sponsored content, or anything unrelated to astrology.

Your zodiac sign is used to personalize the readings you receive. It is not sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

Third parties

We use Google Sheets (via Google Apps Script) to store subscriber data. This means your email and sign are held on Google's servers under Google's infrastructure. We do not use any third-party email marketing platforms, analytics services, or advertising networks.

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your information with anyone.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any newsletter with "unsubscribe" in the subject line. You can also request that we delete your data entirely by emailing us directly. We will honor all such requests within 7 days.
